      Was this an organic consultation or a campaign?

      Mass campaigns can dominate raw counts and distort any aggregate reading. Each submission's free text was normalised and compared (TF-IDF cosine, multilingual embeddings being unavailable in this environment) to detect verbatim duplicates and template families.
